The Biltmore House

Experience America’s Largest Home®, as magnificent today as it was more than a century ago. Your self-guided house visit spans three floors and the basement of the luxurious family home of George and Edith Vanderbilt. Explore the stories of the Vanderbilt family, their guests, and employees through displays of vintage clothing, accessories, art, furniture, and more.

Gardens & Grounds

Stroll acres of formal and informal gardens designed to America’s foremost landscape architect, Frederick Law Olmsted. From the gardens’ year-round beauty to the majesty of the nation’s first managed forest, Biltmore’s breathtaking landscapes are a tribute to Olmsted’s genius.

Winery

Your daytime admission to Biltmore includes a complimentary wine tasting—an introduction to our distinctive portfolio of multi-award-winning reds, whites, and rosés. Our knowledgeable wine hosts are on hand to guide your selections from among more than 20 handcrafted vintages.

Expand your palate with our tours and tastings. Go behind the scenes at the Biltmore Winery with a guided tour of our production facility, or delight in the classic pairing of red wine and chocolate. Our exclusive Vine to Wine Vineyard Tour & Tasting combines an excursion to our vineyards on the estate’s west side—an area that most guests never see—and a walking production tour with wine tastings throughout the experience.

Antler Hill Village

A casual and relaxing way to extend your Biltmore experience, Antler Hill Village® connects the estate’s past and present with dining, shopping, exhibits, the Farmyard, and more. Access to Antler Hill Village is included in your Biltmore daytime admission.

As part of your Biltmore experience, explore Antler Hill Village before or after a self-guided visit of Biltmore House. Enjoy lunch, dinner, a tasty snack, or a glass of wine before treating yourself to an overnight stay at our charming Village Hotel.

Live music from the Bandstand (offered seasonally), including our popular Live After Five series, and ice cream from the nearby Creamery provide the perfect excuse to lie back in the grass, take in the scenery, and unwind after your day on the estate.
